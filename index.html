About us
20+Years

Built on integrity. Proven through performance.

Established in 2004, Aruamz Associates is a premier multidisciplinary firm specializing in Customs Clearance, Freight Forwarding, International Logistics, Trade Consultancy, and Legal Services.

Founded by Mr. Syed Amjad Ali Shah, the firm has built an enduring reputation for integrity, professionalism, and excellence. With decades of industry experience and a deep understanding of regulatory and commercial frameworks, we provide practical, efficient, and results-driven solutions to businesses and individuals in Pakistan and international markets.

Today, Aruamz Associates stands as a trusted partner for organizations seeking seamless trade facilitation, strategic legal guidance, and comprehensive logistics solutions.
